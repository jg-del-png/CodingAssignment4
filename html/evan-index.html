Cinnamon Roll Delight

Cinnamon Roll

Recipe source: OnlyAllRecipes Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted softened butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 4 ounces softened cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups powedered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm milk and active dry yeast. Stir gently and let sit for 5 minutes until mixture turns frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine granulated sugar, yeast mixture, melted butter, eggs, and salt. Gradually add flour and mix until soft dough forms.
  3. Turn dough onto floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth.
  4. Place dough in greased bowl, cover with damp cloth, and let it rise in warm place for an hour until it's size doubles.
  5. In a small bowl, combine softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon. Mix until combined.
  6. Punch down risen dough and turn it out on floured surface. Roll into large rectangle.
  7. Spread cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over rolled-out dough, leaving small border around edges.
  8. Starting from long edge, roll dough into a log. Pinch seam to seal.
  9. Use a knife to cut the dough log into 12-15 pieces.
  10. Place rolls cut-side up in a greased baking pan.
  11. Cover the pan with a damp cloth and let rolls rise for 30-45 minutes.

  13. Bake the rolls in oven for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  14. Prepare the icing by beating the softened cream cheese and butter in a bowl until smooth. Gradually add powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk.
  15. Once rolls are out of oven, let them cool for a few minutes before adding the icing.
