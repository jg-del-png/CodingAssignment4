Elephant Ears

Recipe by: This Silly Girl's Kitchen — Elephant Ears Recipe

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Fine sea salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Peanut oil for frying

=

Directions

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  2. Cut in the unsalted butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add milk and mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into desired shapes.
  5. Heat peanut oil to frying temperature and fry the dough until golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towels, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar, and serve warm.
