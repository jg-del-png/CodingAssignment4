Pavlo's Kimchi Recipe

Kimchi (Traditional Korean Fermented Cabbage)

Recipe by: Pavlo | Source: HotCooking.co.uk

Ingredients

  • Napa cabbage
  • Non-iodized salt
  • Garlic cloves
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Gochugaru (Korean chilli powder)
  • Sriracha or gochujang
  • Sugar
  • Rice vinegar
  • Spring onions
  • Carrot and radishes

Directions

  1. Cut and salt the cabbage, soak for a few hours.
  2. Make the chilli paste with garlic, ginger, sauces and spices.
  3. Mix all vegetables with the chilli paste.
  4. Pack into a clean jar and ferment at room temperature.
